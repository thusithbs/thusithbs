About Us

Pinnacle Horizons (Private) Limited is a Sri Lanka–based export sourcing, quality assurance, and supply management company specializing in coconut shell charcoal and coconut shell charcoal briquettes for BBQ, shisha (hookah), and industrial use. We work with a network of vetted coconut shell charcoal producers across Sri Lanka, enabling us to provide stable supply volumes, consistent product specifications, and export-grade quality to European and international buyers. All products supplied under Pinnacle Horizons are centrally inspected, standardized, and consolidated prior to shipment to ensure consistency across batches, regardless of the production source.
